# Role Playbooks (Business)

This document provides practical “what to click first” guidance per role.

> Note: Exact buttons/labels may evolve. Use the sidebar navigation as the starting point.

## Executive / Leadership

**Goal:** monitor business outcomes, understand exceptions, approve the plan.

1. **Dashboard**
   - Review summary KPIs and alerts.
   - Check the current S&OP cycle status.
2. **KPI**
   - Review KPI trends vs targets (service, inventory, financial).
3. **Approvals**
   - If plans/scenarios are submitted, open the relevant module:
     - Demand plan approvals: **Demand**
     - Supply plan approvals: **Supply**
     - Scenario approvals: **Scenarios**
   - Approve/reject with comments.
4. **S&OP Cycle**
   - Confirm final executive step is complete.
   - Ensure key decisions are recorded.

## S&OP Coordinator

**Goal:** run the monthly 5-step process and maintain accountability.

1. **S&OP Cycle**
   - Create/manage the active cycle.
   - Assign owners/due dates.
   - Advance steps as meetings complete.
2. **Dashboard**
   - Monitor status/alerts and follow up on blockers.

## Demand Planner

**Goal:** create a demand plan and shepherd it through approval.

1. **Forecasting**
   - Generate a baseline forecast (pick model or auto-select).
2. **Demand**
   - Create demand plans (product + period).
   - Apply adjustments and add notes.
   - Submit for approval.
3. **Forecasting (Accuracy/Anomalies)**
   - Use accuracy metrics to validate model performance.
   - Use anomalies to investigate spikes/drops.

## Supply Planner

**Goal:** reconcile supply with demand and publish a feasible supply plan.

1. **Supply**
   - Create/update supply plans.
   - Capture capacity and constraints.
2. **Gap analysis**
   - Compare demand vs supply for a selected period.
   - Define mitigation actions for shortages/excess.
3. **Submit** supply plan for approval.

## Inventory Manager

**Goal:** keep inventory healthy (avoid stockouts and excess).

1. **Inventory**
   - Review inventory health summary and alerts.
   - Drill into critical/low/excess SKUs.
2. **Update inventory policy** (where allowed)
   - Safety stock, reorder point, max stock.
3. Provide inputs for Pre-S&OP decisions (expedite, rebalance, defer buys).

## Finance Analyst

**Goal:** quantify tradeoffs and validate financial impacts.

1. **Scenarios**
   - Create a scenario (demand %, capacity %, lead time, price changes, etc.).
   - Run it to compute results.
2. **Compare scenarios**
   - Select candidate scenarios and compare side-by-side.
3. Submit scenario for approval.

## Admin

**Goal:** ensure master data and access control are correct.

1. **Products**
   - Maintain products and categories.
2. **User access/roles**
   - Create users and assign roles.
3. Support data hygiene needed for forecasting and reporting.
